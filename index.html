Total Collapse - Unleash the Power of Matching!

Dive into the vibrant world of Block Blast: Total Collapse! Match two or more blocks of the same color to clear levels and unlock thrilling boosters like line blasts, color blasts, and bombs. With intuitive gameplay, beautiful graphics, and hundreds of challenging levels, this game promises endless fun and excitement for players of all ages!
